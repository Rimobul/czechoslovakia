# Beer as Part of National Identity

Beer in both countries is more than just a drink – it's part of cultural heritage and social life. Czechs hold the world record for beer consumption per capita, while Slovaks have their own rich brewing tradition.

## Czech Beer

The Czech Republic is the birthplace of lagers as we know them today. Pilsner Urquell from 1842 changed the beer world forever.

### Most Famous Czech Breweries

- **Pilsner Urquell** – the original pale lager
- **Budweiser Budvar** – České Budějovice
- **Staropramen** – Prague brewery
- **Kozel** – Velkopopovický Kozel

## Slovak Beer

Slovakia has a smaller but dynamically growing brewing scene. In recent years, there has been a boom in craft breweries.

### Most Famous Slovak Breweries

- **Zlatý Bažant** – most widespread brand
- **Šariš** – East Slovak tradition
- **Urpiner** – Banská Bystrica
- **Kaltenecker** – craft brewery

## Beer Statistics

## Conclusion

Both countries share a love of beer, but each has its unique approach. While Czechs are proud of their beer tradition, Slovaks are quickly catching up with modern craft breweries.
